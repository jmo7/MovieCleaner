MovieCleaner Help

Opening and Closing the MovieCleaner dialog

When a movie on a supported site (currently Netflix and Amazon) is opened, MovieCleaner starts automatically and opens the main MovieCleaner Dialog.

To close the dialog, do one of the following:

  • If you are being presented with a choice in the main message area of the dialog (such as choosing whether to use a filter or not) choose "Ok" or "Cancel".
  • Click the ❭❭ button in the upper right corner of the MovieCleaner dialog.
    Note that if there are currently buttons in the message area of the dialog, clicking this will essentially put that choice on hold.

To re-open the dialog:

  • Click MovieCleaner's extension icon in the row of extension icons to the right of the browser's address bar.

Pausing and Unpausing your Video

Whenever the main MovieCleaner Dialog opens, the video you are watching is paused. Whenever the dialog is closed, the video returns to playing.

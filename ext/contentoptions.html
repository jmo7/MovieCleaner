Sometimes the timing information contained in the Closed Caption files is off slightly. Using the first slider below, you can tell MovieCleaner to start its mute a small amount of time before or after what the Closed Caption file says. Using the second slider you can change the length of time that MovieCleaner will mute the video. The numbering above the sliders is in seconds.
